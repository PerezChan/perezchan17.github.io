Problem Statement

Design and Innovate on a Blockchain-based Fintech Application that incorporates Web3 and Gamification

"FinanceValley" - Introducing Personal Finance to Youths

"FinanceValley" is a theoretical prototype that I came up with for my Business Innovation Project. The main concept is gamifying managing personal finances and potentially teach the youth about investments through web3.0

This prototype draws heavy inspiration from the popular pixel game "Stardew Valley" but incorporating cryptocurrency and daily rewards that incentivise users to play the game and subsequently learn about their personal finances.

Crypto Tokens that can be earned through playing the game

Another concept is incorporating tokens that can be traded for monetary value. Through daily tasks and achievements, the users will earn these tokens.

Main Takeaways and Learning Points

"FinanceValley" was a very basic concept and visual prototype which has much potential that can be further looked into with the right people who believes in the idea. This project was recommended by my lecturer to present for the PolyFintech Hackathon in 2022 held in Singapore Polytechnic. Being my first hackathon, I learnt a tremendous amount and networked with many like-minded individuals, many who has equally or even better ideas and deeper knowledge in the game development and finance field.
