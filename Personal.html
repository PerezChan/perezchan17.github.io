3D Designing Chess Pieces with OnShape

During my studies in NYP, I had an extra-curricular course on 3D Printing using a free software OnShape. This peeked my interest in 3D designing, as a picky person I could not find any chess sets that I found worth buying so why not make my own?


The main idea behind my design is to have a circular base which transformed into a hexagonal shape upwards. I am quite proud of my design especially the rook although to be my own worst critic I think the knight can be improved. That would require more technical skills in 3D designing which I hope to improve when I have more free time someday.

During my NFT days, I was trying to get whitelisted for a project. In order to get their attention I decided to learn some basic animation with legos and I tried my best to recreate their art with my own.
