Procter & Gamble Internship

Procter & Gamble Singapore Pte. Ltd

•

2022 - 2023

Procter & Gamble has emerged as a global powerhouse in consumer goods, boasting a diverse portfolio of household, personal care, and hygiene brands. With a plethora of brands under its umbrella, effective data handling stands as a cornerstone of P&G's success. Through meticulous data analysis, P&G navigates market trends, consumer preferences, and product performance with precision, ensuring the continued relevance and success of brands like Tide, Crest, and Pampers.

This commitment to data-driven decision-making enables P&G to innovate, optimize, and sustain its market leadership, emphasizing the critical role of data management in driving the company's achievements and maintaining its competitive edge in the dynamic consumer goods landscape.

Main Objective

Develop an Interactive Dashboard to uncover Market Insights on House Products as compared to Competitor Products using Consumer Studies' Data.

Visualisations

Due to the sensitive nature of the Consumer Study Data, I am not permitted to showcase all of my visualisations

Results

After working closely with the Hair Care team in P&G, I am proud of my achievements in Dashboard Creation, Automating Processes to improve efficiency and Leading the team towards the right direction backed by data-driven visualisations.

Using excel macros and google translate, I was able to auto-translate all their consumer study data from the local language to English in under 5 minutes. When done manually this would take two-four working days

The Interactive Dashboards allowed the team to drill down deeper into what is the root cause of certain products out-performing or under-performing. They had the ability to select fragrances of their choice and the closest products with said fragrance will be highlighted and shown on a heat map and stacked bar graph, along with their number of sales

Main Takeaways and Learning Points

This Opportunity to work in a Multi-National Corporation with so many products, gave me real world experience in handling data of those products and their competitors. The entire process from raw data to dashboard creation and deployment had to be self-learnt under the guidance of my supervisor and manager.

Open Communication and Teamwork is Key to Success
Before my Internship, I was a very independent student who tend to stray away from asking questions and clarifying doubts. Through the six months I learnt that asking questions are incredibly beneficial to not just myself but my team. This eliminates any doubts and potential bottlenecks when we are actually doing our work.

Identifying and pursuing low-hanging fruit can be very… fruitful.
I was very enthusiastic to use complicated charts and visualisations to impress my team. However, I understood over time that not everyone is data-literate and sometimes Less is More. Sometimes a bar or pie chart is the most concise way to get my point across.

I am immensely grateful to have the opportunity to intern at P&G. The work culture is amazing, giving my much flexibility and creative freedom to learn through mistakes without judgement. I would like to thank my Manager, Oku-San and my Supervisor, Deepa for guiding my within my six-months. :)
